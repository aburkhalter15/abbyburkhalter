About Abby

As a web developer, I seek to turn ideas into realities. It is my goal to deliver a product to my clients that exceed their expectations of what their website should be. I always try to make the client happy by going the extra mile because my reputation is important to me, and I want to be known for being a pleasure to work with. A website is not just a portal to lead someone to information, but it is a tool that should be used to help people, be it to grow their business, to spread information, or to connect other people. I find that coding a website is enjoyable to me because I like to piece everything together from scratch. There is something magical about pressing buttons and seeing things come together. It is also challenging at times because sometimes objects on webpages do not always behave the way you expect them to, in which case troubleshooting comes in. Troubleshooting is a major part of the job, and it is rewarding when you reach a solution. The gratifying feeling of a smooth running website after much hard work will never get old for me.
